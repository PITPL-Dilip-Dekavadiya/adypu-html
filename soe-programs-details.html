B.Tech - Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

The B. Tech. Computer Engineering Program aims providing a systematic foundation in computer engineering. The core emphasis is on making candidate capable to understand, design and analysis the problem; delivered through a project based approach. Students are trained in such a way that they can design the engineering solutions for the existing real-world computing problems.

Students are skilled for core of computer science course which ensures a strong foundation in computer engineering. Building on this foundation, students explore a variety of specializations.

Programming machines is the main focus of the B.Tech undergraduate degree in artificial intelligence and machine learning. In essence, it enables the candidate to interact with the machine in its own speech. The candidate must therefore master these particular languages as part of the programme in order to tailor the functionality of machines.

B.Tech Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

Duration: Full time, 4 years

Eligibility: XII'th Science with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.
