Pune
A city of Dreams

Pleasant weather, a
beautiful city, and warm
people – you are sure to
fall in love with Pune.

If you dreamed of a place where the right-brained dreamers inspired the left-brained doers – Pune would be the perfect place. Pune is a city where the creative minds of the world live side by side with the scientific and technological innovators of tomorrow.

Pune has been a knowledge capital of India with a glorious past, an excellent present and a great future. Located in the Sahayadri Hills, near the west coast of India, Pune is a fascinating city with a beautiful climate all year round. It is the seventh largest city in India, has the sixth largest metropolitan economy and the highest per capita income in India, with the least income disparity between the rich and poor.

Known as
Punya-nagri

  • City of virtuous deeds
  • The Oxford of the East
  • Thriving cultural
  • Educational hub
  • Young, cosmopolitan city

It has for long been a seat of learning, industrial activity, and a vibrant cultural city. Once a seat of the Peshwas, a power that ruled India, it is the karma bhoomi of social reformers like Gopal Ganesh Agharkar, Mahatma Phule, Babasaheb Ambedkar, Dhondo Keshav Karve and many more. The city was at the forefront of the freedom struggle with great freedom fighters such as Senapati Bapat, Bal Gangadhar (Lokmanya) Tilak, Vinayak (Veer) Savarkar making it their base.

Pune has kept the great traditions of Indian classical music, dance and drama alive and thriving, with performers of international repute and a connoisseur audience. Home to unique events like the Sawai Gandharv Music Festival, Pune International Film Festival, Shaniwar Wada Festival and the Pune Ganesh Festival, it is a city where there is something of interest, serving diverse choices, throughout the year. The city also boasts an eclectic collection of shops, restaurants, coffee houses and a vibrant art scene.

Pune provides
the perfect balance
of work and play.

Pune holds in its heart a lot of youthful energy and independent spirit with a rich mix of traditional and contemporary beliefs and values. The city welcomes people from far and wide, providing them with a safe 'home-like' environment and a large number of international and out station students call this city home.
