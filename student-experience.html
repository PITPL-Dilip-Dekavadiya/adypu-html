Student
Experience

Unique learning experience
to explore new ways of
generating cutting-edge
solutions using creativity
and innovation

The emphasis at ADYPU is always on creating an exciting and charged environment, where a mix of youthful vibrancy with experience & maturity, global viewpoint with local reality, emerging technology with traditional wisdom, and existing knowledge with rebellion and constant questioning will lead to an exciting journey throughout the programs. In this turbulent, get-real economy, the advantage goes to those who out-imagine and out-create their competitors… The real challenge lies in getting better and better at a different thing: devising solutions to wickedly difficult problems. Get this unique advantage over others, innovate rather than replicate.

Student Handbook
The Ajeenkya DY Patil University is situated at the DY Patil Knowledge City. All the necessary facilities are provided on campus. The classrooms are equipped with state-of-the art video projection systems and ergonomically suited furniture.

100

Acres campus with aesthetic landscaping and beautiful architecture

300

Seat auditorium, aesthetically designed studios and classrooms

The campus life is a combination of serious academics with lots of fun. You will find an extensive range of cultural and social activities on campus, which will make learning a joy and your stay on the campus a memorable time. There is something for everyone, whether you are interested in sports or art. The aim is to enhance the learning environment by creating opportunities for students to engage in social and cultural pursuits.

Apart from the high curricular standards, we promote extra-curricular activities with the same vigor. We provide appropriate platform for the students to display many other facets of their personality, which otherwise may not be explored. Extra-curricular activities include drama, singing, skits, elocution, debating, sports etc.

SoHM Chef’s Club

The ADYPU-SHM Chefs Club organizes events and activities to give today’s youth a platform to showcase their culinary talent. Regular activities are organized at ADYPU-SHM for students by the organizing committee. Organizing Committee members are elected by the students of each semester from each course. Activities are scheduled throughout the year to boost the talents of students leading to their overall development.

Our most popular events include Chefs Competition, International Chefs day, Worlds Breads day, International Theme Lunch & Brunch, Traditional Cake Mixing Ceremony, Ajeenkya Food Festival, Master Classes of Culinary Cuisines, Culinary Workshop/Practical, Food Donation activity, Chairman Sir Birthday, Shivaji Market Visits, Chocolate Factory Visit, Culinary Experts Talks Sessions, Veg. & Fruit Carving workshop etc.

“Happiness travels from Stomach to Humans Brain” which is magic of good chef.

Bridging the gap
between the university
and the learner

Our division embraces the vision of Ajeenkya DY Patil University as a home for the mind, the heart and the spirit, and our goal is student success. The Division of Student Services strives to educate the whole person and to help our students be successful in the classroom and on the playing fields, in their relationships with faculty and with friends, and in their growth and development toward a chosen career. We welcome students in crisis, students at academic crossroads, students who are questioning their personal choices, and students who are seeking involvement in the community that is Ajeenkya DY Patil University.

Student Services Department will strive to provide the right information at the right time, to facilitate and provide support services for the divergent learner population, to redress grievances at every stage and thus bridge the gap between the university and the learner.

Meet the Team

Head – Student Services Division
Ms. Priya Abraham

Ms. Priya Abraham heads the Student Services Division with full passion and commitment. With a diverse and extensive experience of 6 years with the University, she act as a pillar to the division of Student Services.Priya started as an Academic Advisor at Ajeenkya DY PatilUniversity.

Ms. Priya is a commerce graduate from K J Somaiya college of Arts and Commerce, Mumbai. She also has 4 years of experience for working with people from different nationalities as a communication expert.

Student Counselor
Ms. Sneha Raizada.

Ms. Sneha Raizada is a Psychologist/Counsellor with more than five years of varied experience working with various national and international Schools and Institutes across the state. Ms. Raizada has completed her Counselling Psychology from Pune University. She is also so a certified Energik healer and is a certified Adult Child abuse survivor trainer.

She has experience working on multiple cases including children, teens, adults, and the elderly in different national and international institutions. She has worked extensively in the areas of child and adolescent psychology, interpersonal relationships, relationship concerns, suicidal ideations, learning difficulties, anxiety, attention defecate hyperactivity disorder, and child abuse.
