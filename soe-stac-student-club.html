Achievements
BITS, Hyderabad Campus
Atmos 2017 (27th - 29th october 2017)

The students of school of Engineering associated with Student Technical Activity Club (STAC), ADYPU participated in various robotic competitions at ATMOS-17, the National Level Competition organized by BITS-Hyderabad Campus from 27-10-2017 to 29-10-2017.The team consisted of 15 students who
participated in the following robotic events.
1. Line Following Robots
2. Bomb Squad
3. I-Navigate

1st Place - Bomb Squad, 2nd Place - I-Navigate

The team participated in Bomb Squad event stood FIRST and was rewarded with Rs.20,000.00 cash prize. The I-Navigate team ranked SECOND and received a cash prize of Rs.6000.00.
