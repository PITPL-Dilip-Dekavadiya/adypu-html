HOD's Message

From unknown faces to buddies, from stupid students to pet ones, from labs to projects, from participants to winners, from followers to leaders, from job seekers to entrepreneurs….. It’s one of life’s great joys to see all of you accomplishing what you’ve been aspiring for and worked toward. Though, it is hard to see you go from the SOE family, but proud to see you leaving behind a great legacy. It is an honor and pride for us to witness you as our graduating student. As alumni, you are the face and the brand ambassador of ADYPU who purveys the words of their alma mater. Your generous efforts bring in worthy resources and attract global aspirants. We recognize the prospective role of our alumni in shaping the future of the University. It is through them that the University aspires to be one of the global hubs of higher education.

We have Pan-ADYPU alumni forum to kindle the feeling of nostalgia amongst the alumni and provide an avenue to our alumni for the pride and gratification of contributing to the growth of the University. You all are cordially invited to connect with us and stay engaged with the ADYPU-SOE Alumni Family. As our alumni, I urge your wholehearted involvement in your alma mater affairs. Your association will definitely motivate and inspire your young successors. I’m sure you will take this pride to be a part of the ADYPU alumni family and stay in touch with us. There are very few moments in life which give you moments for life. The journey of graduation is one of them. We will always cherish the journey we had with you.

Thanks for those memories that we shared together!
Congratulations and best wishes for your next adventure!

Alumni President's Message

The 4 years at ADYPU are beyond words. It was during these years that our personalities were moulded that would help us face the numorous obstacles our futures would throw at us. At ADYPU, Most importance is given to the all round development of the students. Students, here, get the taste of industry based learning through projects, internships as well as through their entrepreneurial ventures which would help them excel in all kinds of fields. By getting industry exposure, students are made to understand the various issues in our industries as well as solve them in the most apt way possible and hence contribute to the society. Students joining here would definitely learn alot and prosper in their careers whilst collecting a myriad of cherishable memories from this campus. Our only advice “Never Quench your thirst for knowledge and take life as it comes”.

Alumni Meet
2022

Alumni meet was conducted on 8th of April 2022 at the University campus. Around 200 alumni attended the meet from all over India. The event was graced by the honorable vice chancellor shri Hridaysh Deshpande sir. The objective was to foster a sense of community among alumni, while awareness about Alumni society and its contribution. “The after party” was a SUNBURN event followed by dinner, which achieved its aim of informal interaction among alumni and students.

Alumni Interaction
2022

An institution's alumni are the reflection of its past, representation of its present and a link to its future. Alumni interaction was conducted on the event of induction for the first year students on 8th of August 2022. The interaction was designed to guide and motivate the students to take up the correct careers in the right spirit. The alumni invited were Ms. Pallavi Saha (CEO, Co-founder Rigbetels Lab), Mr. Suraj Ravi (Robotics system engineer at Unbox Robotics), Mr. Ashutosh Zagade (CEO, SCADS Technology), Mr. Neelabja Shah (CEO, Shride India Pvt. Ltd.), Mr. Nikhil Mishra (M.S. in Rutgers University, New Jersey), Mr. Aditya Akhade (M.S. In University of Leeds, UK), Ms. Shifa Malik and Ms. Vishakha Pasalkar (Engineers at Alscripts). The evening was hosted by Vikram Tomer and organised by Dr. Ipsita Swain (in-charge, alumni association). The invited alumni shared their experiences and challenges in the panel discussion.
