Our Logo

The Eagle with the spread wings denotes the flight towards the sky that is consistently Soaring and rising higher.

The Saluting Elephants signify the strength of the Team members and also abundance.

The Green Earth and the Green Vines in the background represent the Eco friendliness of the Group.

The Rising Sun signifies the source of all life and light. The word “AJEENKYA” on the flying banner means Undefeated, Unconquerable and Victorious.

We are a world-class university that prepares students for the global market, with a single-minded focus on fostering growth through a spirit of innovation.
