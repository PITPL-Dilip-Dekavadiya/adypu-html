Ajeenkya DY Patil University (ADYPU), an innovation university offers Ph.D. programmes through School of Engineering, School of Law, School of Design, School of Management, and School of Film & Media. ADYPU Doctoral Studies is an interdisciplinary pursuit of solving the wicked problems that brings in social or economic benefits for the stakeholders.

Candidate shall be trained to:

  • Master the research skills and techniques.
  • Conduct research with scholarly integrity.
  • Contribute to original research of an academic standard which is inter- disciplinary in nature.
  • Critically analyze and synthesize innovative and complex research ideas.
  • Communicate with the peers about his or her areas of expertise.

Types of ADYPU
Ph.D. Programs

ADYPU will offer two kinds of Doctoral Programs suited for both academicians as well as practitioners across various multi-disciplinary courses offered by different ADYPU Schools.

Ph.D. Program by Research Ph.D. Program by Practice
For whom it is suitable?

This Program is ideally for post graduates with aptitude for research skills. It is for those who have no work-experience or wishes to pursue their careers in academics.

The Practice Doctorate Program is suited ideally for working professionals with deep professional experience of more than three years and above of practice-based experience.

Submission of Dissertation

Dissertation is a single monograph with clearly outlined findings that indicate definitive solutions to the problem studied.

Dissertation is a single monograph along with a tangible outcome in form of a product, technology, artifact or a design that is innovative and first of its kind.

Duration of the ADYPU
Ph.D. Program

Ph.D. programs shall be for a minimum duration of three years, including course work and a maximum of six years.

Extension beyond the above limits will be governed by the decision of Academic Council of the ADYPU on a case by case basis.

The women candidates and Persons with Disability (more than 40% disability) may be allowed a relaxation of two years for Ph.D.in the maximum duration. In addition, the women candidates may be provided Maternity Leave/Child Care Leave once in the entire duration of Ph.D. for up to 240 days.

Minimum Eligibility Criteria

ADYPU
Ph.D. Program

The minimum eligibility criteria required for admission to the Ph.D. Program is a degree in any one of the areas given below:

  • Masters in Technology or Engineering
  • Masters in Business Administration
  • One/Two years of LLM program with either at least five years of integrated LLB degree after 10+2 examination or three years LLB degree after 10+2+3 examination.
  • Masters in Media & Communication
  • Masters in Design
  • Masters in Hotel Management
  • M.Phil

The candidates seeking admission to the ADYPU Ph.D. programs shall have requisite grades as shown below:

  • A Master’s degree with at least 55 percent marks in aggregate or equivalent. Grade B in the UGC seven-point scale (or an equivalent grade in a point scale wherever grading system is followed).

Ph.D. Program
by Practice

Persons who have the minimum prescribed qualifications and are in service in any of the following establishments shall be eligible for admission to the Practice-based Ph.D. program as sponsored candidates:

  • Defence or other ministries of the Government of India or any other government organizations
  • Established research and development organizations
  • Autonomous bodies and public undertakings
  • Reputed industries as may be recognized by the University

These programs are designed for mid-level practitioners (such as business leaders, engineers, lawyers, or scientists) as well as scholars who are working at the boundaries of academic and practice-oriented work. As a participant of the doctorate program, the doctoral candidates are expected to translate research results into beneficial policies, programs, and solutions.

To pursue a Ph.D. Program by Practice the applicant needs to have a minimum of three years of job experience in the reputed organization in the respective discipline in addition to the basic eligibility required for a Ph.D. Program.

Encourage individual and multi-disciplinary research excellence.

ADYPU
Research
Strategy

At ADYPU, our endeavour towards our doctoral programs is to recognise that academic disciplines and interdisciplinary studies provide the richest and most compelling environment to support and develop individual experience.

In this exciting era of Industry 4.0—business corporations, government and non-profit entities are deeply engaged in discovering new business models and digital technologies to boost productivity as well as to build a more sustainable and equitable society.

These challenges demand inputs from diverse disciplines, expertise and inter-disciplinary collaborations to provide path-breaking solutions to ever increasing challenges. It is multi-disciplinary nature of these challenges that motivate the need to identify ADYPU Core Research Themes.

ADYPU's Five Core Research Themes

ADYPU has identified five thematic areas which aim to support responsive and problem-solving interdisciplinary research. Our research themes focuses on being the forerunner in combating the Grand Challenge of creating prosperous communities in an emergent creative and data-driven digital economies.

Through our inter-disciplinary focus, we endeavour to collaborate together industry-academia stakeholders to generate new ideas, good jobs, better earning capacities as well as lay the foundation for successful entrepreneurship.

Each of these themes strives to balance the need to deepen fundamental research as well as to focus on real impact through application to new age societal issues.

The following are the research themes identified by ADYPU:

Technology for
the Future

How human individuals adopt the use of technology for interaction is an interesting field of research. How mankind is equipped to face the rapid changes in the coming future needs to be explored? New dimensions of knowledge with rapid technological trends have the potential to alter social, cultural and communication and physical landscapes.

For instance, Artificial Intelligence and Big Data is transforming and altering the way we live or work. It is raising important questions for the society, governance and economy. The need for research scholars is to comprehend as to how people rethink and integrate real-time data for a more insightful decision making.

A human interaction with complex technologies is an area of great concern across the globe. Through the power of futuristic technologies, future researchers could also explore the possibilities of how future technologies could create magnanimous impact to mankind and augmenting human capabilities in different ways.
